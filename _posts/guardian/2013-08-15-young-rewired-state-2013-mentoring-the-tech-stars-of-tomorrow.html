Young Rewired State (YRS) is a spin-off of the popular hack event group Rewired State, aimed at budding developers and designers aged 18 or below. YRS run a variety of events around the country, but its calendar is dominated by the annual Festival of Code: a week-long hackathon culminating in a weekend of show-and-tell, talks and celebration at Birmingham's Custard Factory.

Four of the Guardian's Digital Development team went along as mentors to see what the next generation of creative techies were up to. This blogpost offers a conversational overview from the Guardian staff about what we learned from our participation in YRS 2013, grouped by topic.
