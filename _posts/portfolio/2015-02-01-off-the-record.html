--- layout: post categories: portfolio title: Off The Record date: 2015-02-01 00:00:00 link: http://offtherecord.club image: portfolio/otr.jpg --- This is a monthly event I run in my home of Birmingham where a bunch of music-loving nerds get together in a pub and listen to an album (matching a theme) together, in full. It's grown from half a dozen geeks in a room to almost 25 people showing up to share music, chat and beers.
