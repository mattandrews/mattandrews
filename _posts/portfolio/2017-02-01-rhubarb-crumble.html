--- layout: post categories: portfolio title: Rhubarb Crumble date: 2017-02-01 00:00:00 link: https://github.com/mattandrews/rhubarb-crumble image: portfolio/rbc.jpg --- An Electron-powered desktop app, this wraps a command line tool for generating mouth shapes from audio files and animates them using images of lips. This was for a BBC Digital Guerrillas project to output quick animated video without manual lipsyncing work.
