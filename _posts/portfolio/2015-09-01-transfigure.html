--- layout: post categories: portfolio title: Transfigure date: 2015-09-01 00:00:00 link: http://transfigure.bbcdigitalguerrillas.co.uk/ image: portfolio/tf.jpg --- Transfigure was a BBC Digital Guerrillas project which took currency values from webpages (eg. £6.99, $120m, €843,422) and converted them to their equivalents in silly, topical values (eg. Freddo bars, footballer salaries, etc). I developed it from a browser extension into a webapp.
