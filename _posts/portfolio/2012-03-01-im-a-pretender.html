--- layout: post categories: portfolio title: I'm a Pretender date: 2012-03-01 00:00:00 link: http://threechords.org/pretender/ image: portfolio/iap.jpg --- I'm A Pretender is a print zine I've sporadically published since the mid-2000s when I was at university. It's a mix of prose writing and occasional forays into poetry (less so as I finished university), mixed with more editorial features and all printed on recycled paper in black-and-white.
