--- layout: post categories: portfolio title: Who Voted What date: 2012-04-01 00:00:00 link: http://whovotedwhat.co.uk/ image: portfolio/wvw.jpg --- This scratched an itch: for the London Mayoral Elections I had no idea what ward I lived in, but wanted to see how mine had voted. Published election data didn't make this easy, so I built this site to allow postcode searching, and added some "badges" to gamify the whole thing.
